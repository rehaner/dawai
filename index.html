History and Origin

Emerging in late 2017, Ugandan Knuckles is a caricatured rendition of Knuckles the Echidna from the iconic SEGA video game franchise featuring Sonic the Hedgehog. The Rise and Spread

The meme's wildfire spread was primarily ignited within the digital realms of "VRChat", an immersive multiplayer online game. Enthusiasts donned the avatar of Ugandan Knuckles, embarking on quests in virtual terrains, often in groups. The meme's influence was pervasive, marking its footprint across various platforms, from Reddit threads to YouTube compilations.
